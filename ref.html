About us

Our intention with this website is to raise awareness on photoprotection and to provide reliable information on sunscreen. With the increase in sun exposure globally, the effects of UV rays on the skin are not fully understood. Visitors to our website learn about the effects of UV radiation and how it can exacerbate skin disorders like melasma. This knowledge emphasizes the importance of protective and restorative skincare.

One of the most important aspects of our website is the fact that it provides sound advice for correct use of sunscreens. People often don’t know how much to put on, how often to reapply or which part of their body they’re most likely to miss. Offering up step-by-step instructions, the site encourages users to get the most of out of their sunscreen and healthy skin. Product details including the Luxe Organix 5-in-1 Uv Pro Whitening Serum Sunscreen are also provided so that customers will be aware of the available products in the market and to make it clearer for them to decide.

Beyond just sharing skin care for our customers to consider, we also promote environmental stewardship. Our website provides appropriate disposal of sunscreen containers and expired products to increase recycling and decrease pollution. Thus, by visiting the website, our visitors are not just taking care of themselves, but working towards environmental stewardship and sustainability.
