Precautionary Measures

1. Keep the product in a shaded area which maintains a dry environment to protect it from direct sunlight exposure.
Sunscreen protection factor (SPF) becomes less effective when exposed to heat and light.

2. Check the expiration date because sunscreen products remain effective for 12 months after opening the container.

3. Avoid touching your eyes with this product. You should wash your eyes right away with clean skin or acne issues.

4. The product should not be applied to skin areas that are damaged or severely inflamed whenever sunscreen enters your eyes.

5. Perform a patch test before using the product completely when you have sensitive skin.

6. The product should remain inaccessible to children under six years old.
